Most trip planners treat the bike as a last resort. If you already own a bike and use SEPTA or PATCO, carrying it on the train or locking it at the station is often faster and cheaper than the route Google Maps gives you — but no mainstream app shows you that comparison.

Velorail is built to fill that gap. Enter a start and end point and it shows you four ways to make the same trip side by side: walk to transit, carry your bike aboard, lock your bike at the station, or use Indego bikeshare for the first or last mile. Each option shows the real travel time and cost under your specific situation — whether you're an Indego member, whether you have a bike, and whether it's peak hours (when SEPTA bars bikes from trains).

It is a work in progress, covering the Philadelphia region (SEPTA, PATCO, and Indego). If something looks wrong or you want to suggest a feature, send a note.

Prototype. Some fares are estimates (marked ~). Fares and bike-aboard rules reflect published agency policy but verify before a time-sensitive trip.
